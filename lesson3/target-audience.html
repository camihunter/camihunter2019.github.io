Target Audience

The target audience is adults, male or female, from the age of 25-55. They will have an average income, and most will have completed at least some college. They will access this information mostly through their mobile device.

Scenarios

  • I'm getting ready for work, and I'm not sure what the temperature will be today. Should I wear a short sleeve shirt?
  • I need to set my alarm to wake up in the morning. Will I need to add extra time to shovel the driveway?
  • How is the weather for our weekend trip looking?
  • I have a few minutes to sit down. What kind of articles were posted today about the snow storm coming up? Is there anything I can do to prepare?
  • Will I need to pack an umbrella today?

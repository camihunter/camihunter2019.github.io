Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to provide daily updated weather for parts of Idaho. It will be a valuable source for residents in the residing areas Elemental Weather covers. It will also include articles relating to weather events that are in the recent past or near future. This will help connect residents to needs that arise, as well as help them better prepare for weather events.

Domain Name

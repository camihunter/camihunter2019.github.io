School Closures

The first winter storm that lasted over the weekend dropped 11 inches of snow. Some areas received more than 15 inches. This caused many roads to shut down due to the dangerous driving conditions. This resulted in snow day for the the Bear Lake Idaho School District. School will resume on Monday, but many families were enjoying the weather together after the storm was over.

The roads have all been cleared, but watch out for black ice and frozen bridges that could still pose a hazard. The temperature will drop to 5 degrees tomorrow night, and it looks like it may stay close to that for a few nights ahead. Plan on the snow sticking around.
